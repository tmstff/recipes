Pad Thai

★★★★★

Thai

Servings: 1

Ingredients

2 Tsp oil pf your choice

1/2 cup shredded carrots

1/2 cup shredded onions

1/2 cup chopped tomatoes

1 small handful prepared fou

1 tsp minced garlic

Presoaked, prepared rice noodles (80-100 gr dry)

1 Tbsp soy sauce mixture

1 tsp sugar

1 small handful bean sprouts

1 small handful chopped spring onion

1 tsp lime juice

Chili flakes to taste

1 small handful roasted peanuts

Description

Classic Thai Noodles

Directions

Fry Carrots, onions and tomatoes in hot oil
Add tofu, garlic and noodles. Mix well together to combine flavors and add soy sauce and sugar.
Add bean Sprouts, spring onion, lime juice and chili flakes to taste.
Sprinkle with crushed peanuts and serve with peanut sauce.
